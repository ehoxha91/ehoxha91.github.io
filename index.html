Biography

Ejup Hoxha is a Machine Learning Engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS) in New York, USA. He is also a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Jizhong Xiao @ CCNY Robotics Lab, focused on Robotics and Computer Vision. Skillful in Robotics, Control, Visual SLAM, Sensor Fusion, and Non-destructive Evaluation.
